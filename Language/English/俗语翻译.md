1. 兔子不吃窝边草
Never fish in your neighbor's pond.
2. 好马不吃回头草
If you let a fish off, you let it off.
3. 天涯何处无芳草
She is not the only fish in the ocean.
4. 巧妇难为无米之炊
Even the cleverest housewife can't make bread without flour.
